Studio Lumière — Identity

A complete visual identity for a Parisian photography studio exploring the tension between light and shadow.

Brief

The studio needed a visual identity that could hold the duality at the heart of their practice: rigor and poetry, precision and emotion. The name itself — Lumière — became the starting point for the entire system.

Année
2025
Client
Studio Lumière, Paris
Services
Identité visuelle, Direction artistique, Typographie

Approach

Working with a reduced palette — deep black, warm white, and a single precise accent — the identity leans on the tension between negative space and typographic presence. The logotype uses a classical serif, slightly compressed to suggest precision without coldness.

"Light is the beginning of all things. The identity had to carry that weight with lightness."
