Jérémy Rambaud is a French art director based in Angers. Currently completing a Master's degree in Art Direction, he works at the intersection of editorial design, visual identity, and typographic research.

With a focus on the editorial and the typographic, his practice explores how images and text construct meaning together. He is currently looking for collaborations, internships, and opportunities to work on ambitious projects.

Jérémy is a designer, typographer, and art director with a particular interest in cultural institutions, independent publishing, and luxury brands.
